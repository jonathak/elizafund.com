The Eliza Fund

Building biotech companies from breakthrough science.

We don’t just invest in biotechnology companies—we help create them.

The Eliza Fund is a biotechnology venture creation and early-stage investment firm focused on promising life science technologies and companies.

We partner with inventors, entrepreneurs, early-stage companies, universities, technology transfer offices, co-investors, and strategic partners to transform compelling science and technology assets into valuable biotechnology companies.

Our Approach

We help build biotechnology companies through scientific and commercial diligence, licensing and asset acquisition, company formation, executive leadership, development strategy, and early-stage financing. We selectively invest our own capital and work with co-investors to support promising opportunities.

Areas of Interest

We seek opportunities in therapeutics, drug delivery, diagnostics, AI-enabled life sciences, platform technologies, and translational medicine with strong scientific foundations, defensible intellectual property, and meaningful potential to improve human health.

Let's Build Together

We welcome conversations with inventors, entrepreneurs, early-stage companies, universities, investors, and strategic partners seeking to create, finance, or advance the next generation of biotechnology companies.
